# 🎖️ Dictator Mbappé - Meme Documentation

A comprehensive documentation and showcase of the viral **Dictator Mbappé** meme trend that took over social media in 2026.

## 📖 About the Meme

The Dictator Mbappé meme depicts Kylian Mbappé as an authoritarian leader with absolute control over Real Madrid's decisions. The meme involves AI-generated videos, Photoshopped images, and satirical posts claiming Mbappé "decreed" player transfers, manager firings, or match results.

## 📅 Timeline

- **March 2024**: Mohamed Henni incident sparks the idea
- **November 2024**: First dictator-themed edit on Reddit
- **January 2026**: Rumors about Mbappé's influence at Real Madrid
- **March 2026**: 🔥 Viral explosion - 13.2M views on TikTok in 2 weeks
- **April 2026**: Streisand Effect as reports claim Mbappé contacted FIFA

## 🏆 Meme Formats

1. **Video Edits** - AI-generated videos with dramatic voiceovers
2. **Photoshopped Images** - Mbappé as various dictators
3. **Character References** - Comparisons to fictional authoritarian figures
4. **Fake News Posts** - Satirical posts about Mbappé's "decrees"

## 🎯 Why It Resonates

In 2026, the world loves exaggerating celebrity influence. The Dictator Mbappé meme perfectly captures this absurdity by jokingly portraying one player as having absolute control over Real Madrid's decisions. It's absurd humor meets football passion.
